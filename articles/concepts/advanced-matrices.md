<span data-ttu-id="868af-206">Ya sabemos cómo calcular el vectores propios y el vectores propios de una matriz diagonal $ D $ . <span data-ttu-id="868af-208">$$M (U ^ \dagger v) = u ^ \dagger d u (u ^ \dagger v) = U ^ \dagger d (u u ^ \dagger ) v = u ^ \dagger D v = c u ^ \dagger v.$$</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="868af-208">$$M(U^\dagger v) = U^\dagger D U  (U^\dagger v) =U^\dagger D (U  U^\dagger) v = U^\dagger D v = c U^\dagger v.$$</span></span>
